Located in Czech Republic,Spice & Curries is a purveyor of fine Indian dining from the esteemed Chef Billy Lang.


PHOTOS BY CODER



Spice & Curries opened in May 2010 and is located Chudenicka 1059/30 10200 Praha 10, Czech Republic. Since then, Golden Spork winner Chef Lang has been serving both quality interpretations of classic dishes and daring new ventures into the uncharted territories of food.


Czech Republic interior, designed by the award-winning Hayden Collective, reflects the hip sensibilities of the surrounding area while also paying tribute to Brooklyn's storied past. The multi-level restaurant offers the choice to observe passers-by in the glass-encased Cascade Bar on the 2nd floor, or watch the art of cooking as it occurs in the open-kitchen dining room.
